Cyber Pixel Marketing LLC ("Cyber Pixel", "we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website at cyberpixel.ae or contact us about our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information collected automatically when you use our website.

Information you provide:

  • Name, email address, and phone number (when you submit a quotation request or contact form)
  • Project details and service requirements you include in your messages
  • Communications with us via email, WhatsApp, or phone

Information collected automatically:

  • Browser type, operating system, and device information
  • IP address and general geographic location
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring website or source that led you to us

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide quotations for our services

  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations applicable in the UAE

We do not sell your personal information or use it for automated decision-making.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These include:

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling certain cookies may affect some website functionality.
